About Me

Hi there! I'm Kamree Laursen!

I'm from a small town in California called Kingsburg. You've probably never heard of it, but you should know it's the Raisin Capital of the World (don't disrespect the raisins)! Growing up, I became passionate about a number of hobbies, such as the following:

  • Embroidering
  • Traveling
  • Hanging with friends
  • Listening to music
  • Going to concerts
  • Taking pictures!

I've always liked taking pictures. When I was little I would snag my parent's Polaroid and run around the house taking silly pictures before my parents could catch me. In my sophomore year of high school, I took a photography class in high school which further sparked my interest and added to my skill. While the taking that class, my parents got me my own camera (Canon T3i), which further ignited my passion. I know focus my efforts on taking pictures of the 2 things I love most: people and nature (especially sunsets)!

Here are my 3 most common packages:
  1. Engagements
  2. Weddings
  3. Scenic Photos
